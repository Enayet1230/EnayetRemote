Key Features

Strikeout

By right clicking on the text of the answer choice, a line will appear through that choice. You may strike out as many answer choices as you wish. You can undo your strikethrough by right clicking on the answer choice text a second time.

Striking out incorrect options does not select the correct option. You must left-click the option so the radio-button or checkbox is marked to answer a question before proceeding to the next question.

Exhibits

If a question requires you to view additional exhibits, you will see a tab for each exhibit at the top of the screen. Be sure to view all exhibits for the question.

Review

You will have the ability to review the questions in the test before submitting your answers for scoring. The review screen will show questions that you have not answered or have marked.

 Mark

If you would like to mark a question to review it later, check the Mark box at the top of the screen. Uncheck the box to remove the mark flag.

 Time Remaining 10:00

The time remaining will show in the top right corner of your screen. If you would like to hide the time, click on the icon of the clock. Click the icon again to show the time.

 1 of 10

The current question number will also show in the top right corner of your screen. If you would like to hide the current question number, click on the icon to the left. Click the icon again to show the current question number.
